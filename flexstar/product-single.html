FLEXSTAR CLONE LED

The Flexstar Photon Board LED Grow Light is a full-spectrum, high-efficiency LED plant lighting system developed for indoor growers. The rich spectral range is suitable for all stages of plant growth. The small, thin design makes it more suitable for limited space such as grow tents. The Flexstar Photon Board series LED grow lights are available in different sizes and can be used in different situations.
