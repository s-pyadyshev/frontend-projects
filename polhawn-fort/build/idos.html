    A wedding can be grand and formal or small and simple. It’s up to you. Tie the knot within the Fort’s mighty walls or there’s the summerhouse surrounded by the ocean.

    Grand Hall Ceremonies

      An inside wedding can be grand and formal or small and simple. It’s up to you. There are two rooms within the Fort’s mighty walls where you can tie the knot. For maximum wow factor, there’s The Grand Napoleonic Hall which seats up to 120 guests. The Emperors Room is perfect for small and intimate weddings with up to 30 guests. Receptions can be held indoors or in a marquee on the lawn. The choice is yours.

        Ceremonies in our

        Summerhouse

        With the sea echoing all around it’s a natural setting to bring all your love ones together for your ceremony. Our sweeping lawn meanders down and is framed against the perfect sea horizon backdrop.

            Space to chill & room to thrill

            Our outside space can be used in many different ways to best suit your wedding plans, from oversized games and deckchairs to hottubs on the lower lawn one thing for sure is it will make a mark in the memory of anyone who gets to experience it.

            As snug as a bug

            Winter weddings

            It’s easy to see why Polhawn Fort makes a fantastic summer wedding venue, but when it snows it becomes a classic winter wonderland. Even without snow, there’s something comforting about wild winter storms lashing the coastline, while you and your guests stay cosy and warm within the Fort’s thick granite walls.

            See more ideas

                Why go winter, why not?

                Our central heating’s up to the challenge and there’s a roaring fire to gather around. Inside you’ll find plenty of rooms to conduct your whole wedding, from the actual ceremony through to the reception and evening party, making the Fort the perfect venue for a winter wedding, whatever the weather.

                A ceremony that’s

                Just for you

                If you want a discreet wedding you’ve found the right place. Far out on the Rame Peninsula the Fort’s hidden away in near total seclusion. It’s definitely not the kind of location you stumble upon or pop into, asking for directions. The only people at your wedding will be the ones you’ve invited tucked away on your very own getaway land, everything an exclusive wedding venue requires.

                    The perfect match

                    Polhawn Fort is a great venue for same-sex weddings as well as religious and non-religious ceremonies of all kinds. Whatever your plans, the Fort won’t disappoint with its stunning sea views and its own private beach. All ceremonies can be conducted both inside the Fort as well as outside in the gardens overlooking the Bay.
